Bullying Support

Healing from the effects of bullying and building resilience

Understanding the Impact of Bullying

Bullying can have profound and lasting effects on mental health and self-esteem. Whether you're currently experiencing bullying or dealing with the aftermath of past experiences, I provide a safe space to process these experiences and build resilience.

My Approach to Bullying Support

I work with individuals affected by bullying, offering:

  • A safe space to share your experiences without judgment
  • Support for healing from the emotional impact of bullying
  • Strategies for building self-esteem and confidence
  • Help with developing coping skills and boundary-setting
  • Support for addressing anxiety, depression, or trauma related to bullying
  • Guidance for navigating school or workplace bullying situations

Reclaiming Your Power

The effects of bullying can linger long after the incidents themselves. Healing involves processing these experiences, rebuilding self-worth, and developing strategies to protect yourself in the future. You deserve to feel safe, respected, and confident in yourself.
