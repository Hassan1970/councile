Antenatal & Postnatal Support

Support through the challenges of pregnancy and new parenthood

Navigating the Journey to Parenthood

The transition to parenthood is one of life's most significant changes, bringing both joy and challenges. Whether you're expecting your first child or adjusting to life with a new baby, I provide support through this transformative period.

My Approach to Antenatal & Postnatal Support

I work with expecting and new parents, offering:

  • Support for anxiety and worries during pregnancy
  • Help with adjusting to changes in identity and relationships
  • Strategies for managing the emotional changes of postpartum
  • Support for bonding with your baby
  • Counseling for perinatal mood disorders
  • Help with the transition back to work

You Don't Have to Navigate This Alone

Pregnancy and new parenthood can bring unexpected emotional challenges. Whether you're experiencing anxiety, depression, or simply adjusting to your new role, counseling can provide valuable support and strategies to help you thrive during this special time.
