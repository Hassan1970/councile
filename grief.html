Grief & Loss Support

Navigating the complex emotions that come with significant loss

Understanding Grief

Grief is a natural response to loss, but it can be one of the most challenging experiences we face. Whether you've lost a loved one, experienced the end of a relationship, or faced another significant loss, I provide support through this difficult time.

My Approach to Grief & Loss Support

I work with individuals experiencing various types of loss, offering:

  • A safe space to express and process your grief
  • Support for the many emotions of grief: sadness, anger, guilt, and more
  • Help with adjusting to life after loss
  • Strategies for coping with difficult days and triggers
  • Support for complicated grief and trauma
  • Help with finding meaning and rebuilding after loss

There's No "Right" Way to Grieve

Grief is a unique and personal experience. There's no timeline for healing, and no "right" way to grieve. Whether your loss is recent or from years ago, counseling can provide support and understanding as you navigate this challenging journey.
