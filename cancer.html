Cancer Support

Emotional support for patients and families navigating cancer journeys

Understanding the Emotional Impact of Cancer

A cancer diagnosis can be one of the most challenging experiences a person and their family may face. The emotional impact often includes feelings of fear, anxiety, uncertainty, and overwhelm. As a counselor specializing in cancer support, I provide a safe space to process these complex emotions.

My Approach to Cancer Support

I work with both cancer patients and their loved ones, offering:

  • Emotional support throughout diagnosis, treatment, and recovery
  • Coping strategies for managing anxiety and fear
  • Communication techniques for discussing cancer with family members
  • Support for body image issues and changes in self-perception
  • Grief counseling for those facing terminal diagnoses
  • Support for survivors transitioning to life after treatment

You Don't Have to Face Cancer Alone

Whether you're newly diagnosed, undergoing treatment, in recovery, or supporting a loved one with cancer, counseling can provide valuable support. Together, we can develop strategies to manage the emotional challenges and find moments of peace and resilience throughout your journey.
