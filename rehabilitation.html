Rehabilitation Support

Counseling for those adapting to life changes after injury or illness

Adjusting to Life Changes

Recovering from injury, illness, or surgery often involves more than just physical healing. The emotional and psychological adjustments can be just as challenging. I provide support to help you navigate these changes and adapt to your new circumstances.

My Approach to Rehabilitation Support

I work with individuals facing various rehabilitation challenges, offering:

  • Emotional support during the recovery process
  • Coping strategies for dealing with physical limitations
  • Support for identity changes and role adjustments
  • Techniques for managing pain and frustration
  • Assistance with setting realistic goals and expectations
  • Support for caregivers and family members

Finding Strength in Recovery

Rehabilitation is a journey that requires patience, resilience, and support. Whether you're recovering from surgery, adapting to a chronic condition, or adjusting to life after an accident, counseling can help you find strength and develop effective coping strategies.
