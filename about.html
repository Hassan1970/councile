About Talisa Appadu

Registered Counsellor dedicated to your wellbeing

My Approach to Counseling

I am a Registered Counsellor dedicated to providing compassionate, professional psychological support to individuals facing various life challenges. With a client-centered approach, I create a safe, non-judgmental space where you can explore your thoughts and feelings.

My practice is built on the belief that everyone has the capacity for growth and healing, and I am committed to helping you tap into your inner resources to overcome obstacles and improve your quality of life.

I adhere to the highest ethical standards and engage in ongoing professional development to ensure that my clients receive the most effective, evidence-based support.

My Qualifications

  • Registered Counsellor with the Australian Counselling Association
  • Master's Degree in Counseling Psychology
  • Over 10 years of experience in mental health support
  • Specialized training in trauma-informed care
  • Certified in Cognitive Behavioral Therapy
