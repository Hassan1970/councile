My Approach to Chronic Illness Support

I work with individuals managing chronic health conditions, offering:

  • Emotional support for coping with diagnosis and ongoing symptoms
  • Strategies for managing the uncertainty of chronic conditions
  • Support for dealing with changes in identity and life plans
  • Help with communicating about your illness with others
  • Techniques for managing anxiety and depression related to health concerns
  • Support for maintaining relationships while managing health challenges

Finding Balance and Resilience

Living with chronic illness requires developing new coping strategies and finding ways to maintain quality of life despite health challenges. Together, we can work on building resilience, finding meaning, and developing practical strategies for navigating the emotional aspects of your health journey.
